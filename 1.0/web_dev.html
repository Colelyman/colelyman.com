Want a Website?

Then you have come to the right place! I design websites for small businesses and for personal use. My websites are affordable yet are of professional quality.

Types of Web Sites I Design

There are two main types of websites I can design, a site on Google Sites or with a CSS template. There are advantages and disadvantages to both of the types of sites.

Google Sites

One advantage of Google Sites is that the web hosting and the domain name are totally free, so other than the inital cost of designing the site and whether or not you would like a support package, there are no other costs. A disadvantage to Google Sites is that there are limited capabilities in what can go in the site and the over all desgining of the site.

CSS Template

One advantage of CSS templates is that the design possibilities are nearly endless. There are disadvantages to using a CSS template. When using a CSS template the customer has to buy their own domain name and pay for web hosting. Depending on the provider and the domain name these could get fairly costly.
